Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GlobalTime Sync ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information when you use our website at https://globaltime-sync.vercel.app (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using GlobalTime Sync, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

GlobalTime Sync is a free tool that does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit GlobalTime Sync, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Browser type and version — To ensure compatibility and diagnose technical issues.
  • Device type — Desktop, tablet, or mobile, used for responsive design optimization.
  • Referring URL — The website that directed you to GlobalTime Sync.
  • Pages visited and time spent — Aggregated usage analytics to understand how the tool is used.
  • IP address — May be collected by our hosting provider (Vercel) and third-party ad services for security and fraud prevention purposes.

2.3 Usage Data

When you use the "Find Best Meeting Time" feature, the selected cities, working hours, and calculated results may be sent to a Google Sheets endpoint for anonymous, aggregated analytics. This data does not include any personally identifiable information — it consists solely of timezone identifiers, time ranges, and browser metadata (user agent string, browser language).

3. Cookies and Local Storage

3.1 Our Cookies

GlobalTime Sync uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to maintain a simple visitor counter. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. It contains only a numeric count and a date string — no personal information.

3.2 Third-Party Cookies

Our third-party service providers may set cookies on your device:

  • Google AdSense — If enabled, Google may use cookies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your Google ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.
  • Google Analytics — If implemented, Google Analytics uses cookies to collect aggregated, anonymous usage data. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Adsterra — Our primary ad partner may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant advertisements. You can learn more about Adsterra's privacy practices at their website.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the GlobalTime Sync service
  • To understand usage patterns and improve the tool
  • To display advertisements that support the free service
  • To detect and prevent abuse or technical issues

We do not use your information to:

  • Build personal profiles or track individual users
  • Sell or rent data to third parties
  • Send marketing emails or notifications

5. Third-Party Services

GlobalTime Sync integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — Hosting and content delivery. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery. Google may collect IP addresses when fonts are loaded. Google Privacy Policy
  • Adsterra — Advertising. Adsterra Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Advertising (if enabled). Google Ads Policy
  • Google Apps Script / Google Sheets — Anonymous usage analytics.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We do not maintain a user database. Aggregated analytics data stored in Google Sheets is retained indefinitely for trend analysis but contains no personally identifiable information. Browser localStorage data remains on your device until you clear it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — Request information about what data we hold about you (in our case, none that is personally identifiable).
  • Deletion — Clear your browser's localStorage and cookies to remove all locally stored data.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads — Use your browser's cookie settings or visit Google Ad Settings to manage ad personalization.
  • Do Not Track — We respect Do Not Track browser signals where applicable.

8. Children's Privacy

GlobalTime Sync is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of GlobalTime Sync after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our GitHub repository or contact channels listed on the About page.

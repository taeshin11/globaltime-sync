    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does GlobalTime Sync work?

    GlobalTime Sync uses your browser's built-in Intl API to display accurate time zone data for any city in the world. Simply search and add cities to see their current times side by side on an interactive 24-hour timeline.

    Is GlobalTime Sync free to use?

    Yes, GlobalTime Sync is completely free. There are no sign-ups, subscriptions, or hidden fees. All features are available to everyone.

    How do I find the best meeting time across time zones?

    Add all the cities where your team members are located, adjust working hours if needed, then click "Find Best Meeting Time". The tool will calculate overlapping working hours and suggest the best windows.

    How many cities can I compare at once?

    You can compare up to 10 cities simultaneously. Each city shows a live clock with hours, minutes, seconds, date, and UTC offset, plus a visual 24-hour timeline bar.

    Does GlobalTime Sync account for Daylight Saving Time?

    Yes. GlobalTime Sync uses the IANA time zone database through your browser's Intl API, which automatically handles Daylight Saving Time transitions for all supported time zones.

    Can I share my meeting time results?

    Yes! After finding the best meeting time, you can use the copy-to-clipboard button to copy the results and share them with your team via email, chat, or any messaging app.
